Tesla Model 3 · LevoAir Custom Wraps

Brand-grey paint with LevoAir gold accents, a drone emblem on the hood and the official LevoAir wordmark on the doors. Built for the Model 3 (2024+) base Paint Shop wrap template. Four designs — preview them on a car below, then download the wrap file.

How to apply on your Tesla

  1. Download a wrap .png (button on each design).
  2. Format a USB drive (exFAT/FAT32) and create a folder named Wraps at the root.
  3. Copy the PNG file(s) into the Wraps folder (up to 10, each ≤ 1 MB).
  4. In the car: Toybox → Paint Shop → Wraps, then select your LevoAir design.

Mockups are stylised side-profile previews to communicate colour, accent placement, drone and logo — the actual on-car result is rendered by the vehicle's 3D Paint Shop from the wrap PNG. Logo is the official LevoAir wordmark; the door logo is mirrored on the driver side in the texture so it reads correctly on the physical car.
